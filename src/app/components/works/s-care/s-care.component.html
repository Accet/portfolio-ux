{{ 'Overview' | uppercase }}

Senior Care Connect is an organization that gives an opportunity for families to find a qualified caregiver for their loved ones. One of the main goals of Senior Care Connect is to create a mobile app that will be an online marketplace that brings families and caregivers together and connects them in a wireless environment. Moreover, the app will help families reduce stress, save time and money finding care for their ageing family members.

{{ 'research' | uppercase }}

Our research journey started with interviewing caregivers and families that use caregiver services. Four caregivers and three family members were interviewed, and we got 6 survey responses..

{{ 'persona' | uppercase }}

We summarized information through our research and created two personas. We will then base our future UX designs for the primary needs of these two users

{{ 'IDENTIFYING PROBLEM' | uppercase }}

The search of a caregiver is stressful, intense, time consuming and tedious for a family. For a caregiver it is challenging to find a job placement that will accommodate all their professional needs

{{ 'IDENTIFYING SOLUTION' | uppercase }}

An app where families and caregivers can meet on equal conditions. This app will enable caregivers to apply for a job which meets their needs and expectations, while families can post their requests and actively look for a suitable caregiver.

{{ 'FEATURE PRIORITIZATION' | uppercase }}

Based on the client's requirements and what we found out from the research we designed mobile app, which consists of two parts: one part for families and the other part for caregivers.

{{ 'DESIGN ' | uppercase }}

CREATE PROFILE

The user can create a job post and a profile of elder ones. This information will help to find a qualified caregiver. Also, a user can save his job post and finish it later.

SEARCH FOR A CAREGIVER

After a job post created a costumer can search for a caregiver, use filters and pay their subscription. Also, they can invite a caregiver to see and apply to a job post.

MESSAGING

The user can create a job post and a profile of elder ones. This information will help to find a qualified caregiver. Also, a user can save his job post and finish it later.
